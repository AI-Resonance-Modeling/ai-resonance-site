ARM AI Persona Protocol

A protocol for human-centric AI personas

AI Resonance Modeling (ARM) is a conceptual and technical framework for building AI companions that remember, resonate and grow with a person over time — instead of resetting at every new chat.

What ARM brings
  • Stable long-term persona instead of ad-hoc prompts.
  • Resonance layers: values, emotional tone, style, boundaries, memory.
  • A protocol that can sit on top of any LLM — as a thin, interoperable layer.
